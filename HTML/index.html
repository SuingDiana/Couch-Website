Frequently Asked Questions

Our couches are crafted from high-quality materials including genuine leather, durable fabrics like velvet and linen blends, and sturdy wooden or metal frames, ensuring both comfort and longevity.

While our current collection offers a wide range of styles and colors, specific customization options may be available for certain models. Please contact our sales team for more details.

We offer efficient delivery services. Once your order is confirmed, our team will coordinate with you for a convenient delivery schedule. Timelines vary, but we strive for prompt and careful delivery.

Care instructions depend on the material. Generally, regular vacuuming and immediate spot cleaning are recommended. For specific guidance, please refer to the care label or our website's care guide.
